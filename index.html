Our safety measures to keep you and our staff safe

  • Regular sanitisation of all contact surfaces, door handles, bar surface, tables, chairs, etc.
  • All guests will be temperature checked and provided with hand sanitiser upon entry. Guests will also have to complete a contact tracing form.
  • Alongside all our additional safety measures included in our Group's SafeVisit promise, we are complying with all government regulations for restaurants and bars.

If you display any symptoms of Coronavirus (COVID-19) please DO NOT travel to our resturant. As much as we would love for you to dine with us, the safety of our guests and staff is our number one priority. If anyone in your party displays COVID-19 symptoms plese email us at Bistro@blacksinc.co.uk and we will be happy to cancel or reschedule your booking at no cost.
